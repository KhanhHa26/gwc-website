FAQ

Answers before you register.

Do I need a team?

Yes, this is a team-based competition of 2-4 people per team. No worries if you haven't found a team yet, show up solo and we will match you during kickoff based on interests and skills.

Do I have to code?

No. Protothon welcomes first-time coders, designers, storytellers, researchers, and makers ‚Äî curiosity is enough.

Who can attend?

Students from the Tri-Co College: Bryn Mawr, Haverford, and Swarthmore.

What should I bring?

Your laptop, charger, and anything you need to be comfortable during the sprint. We'll provide meals (lunch on Saturday and dinner on Sunday), snacks, and merch.
